Fluffy Pancakes

By Taste of Home.

Test Kitchen approved, with 15 minutes and a non-stick pan you've now got the fluffiest, most delicious pancakes man can create.

Total Time
15 minutes
Cooks
8 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup shortening or butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a small bowl mix to combine the dry ingredients.
  2. Melt your shortening or butter in the pan you intend to cook with, this can be done on medium to low heat. This will both grease the pan while melting your shortening!
  3. After allowing the shortening to cool enough so as not to cook your egg add the shortening, milk, egg and vanilla extract. Mix to combine
  4. On medium heat scoop ¼ of your batter onto the greased pan, cooking until you can see bubbles on the top of your batter. Flip and allow the other side to cook until your pancake is golden brown on both sides
  5. Add the topping of your choice and enjoy!
